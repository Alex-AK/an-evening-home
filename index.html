An Evening at Home.

The Idea.

The idea for An Evening at Home came when a friend shared a story of homemade calzone night - where friends, neighbors, and acquaintances would come together every other week and share an evening in.

The Plan.

An Evening at Home will be hosted every other Thursday at my apartment in West Seattle. There will be food, boardgames, and conversation. Upon request, I will send you event information with directions the night before.
